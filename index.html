New Here?

Not sure what gaming coaching even is? No problem.

Think of it like having a private tutor — but for video games. A coach watches how you play, figures out what's holding you back, and teaches you how to fix it. It's the fastest way to actually improve.

You don't need to be competitive or serious about gaming. Maybe you just want to stop losing to your friends, or you're curious about what all the fuss is about. We meet you exactly where you are.

Learn about our approach
Do I need to be good already to get coaching? +
Absolutely not! Complete beginners are some of our favorite students. There are no bad habits to undo yet, and the improvement curve is incredibly rewarding. We start from zero if needed.
What does a session actually look like? +
Sessions happen over Discord with screen sharing. We'll play together, watch recordings of your games, and talk through specific skills. It's relaxed, friendly, and at your pace — not a bootcamp.
I don't know any gaming terms. Is that okay? +
100% okay. We explain everything in plain language. Our coach has a degree in education specifically because teaching clearly — without jargon — is a real skill. We'll never make you feel dumb for asking.
What makes this different from just watching YouTube? +
YouTube teaches you what to do in general. A coach tells you what YOU specifically are doing wrong and how to fix it. It's the difference between reading a recipe and having a chef in your kitchen with you.
Can I just try one session first? +
Yes! We offer a free 15-minute discovery call before anything else, plus individual drop-in sessions. There's no pressure to commit to a package right away.
